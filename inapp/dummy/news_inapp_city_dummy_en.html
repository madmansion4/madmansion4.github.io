All Feica City apps

Big update!
New features in Feica City apps

2018 / 9 / 1

After a long preparation, we made a big update to the Feica City apps! New features have been added with reference to feedback from our users.

So let's see what's new.

1. Options for Date stamp

Options for Date stamp.

The date stamp option, which was added first to Feica, has been updated the same in the Feica City apps. Now you can specify the date and format you want in the date stamp!

2. Silent mode

Finally added silent mode.

Silent mode is probably the feature most people wanted. If you turn on the silent mode in the settings, you can shoot without the sound! However, keep in mind that the photo quality will deteriorate!

3. New effect < Shiny >

<Shiny> is only available in Feica Busan and Kyoto.

New effect < Shiny > have been added to Feica Busan and Kyoto. < Shiny > Makes a dreamy picture by spreading the light.

4. 1:1 shooting ratio & Self-timer

The top menu in the camera has been changed.

Now you can choose 3:4 and 1:1 ratio when shooting. And also can use a self-timer.

5. Mirror mode

Mirror mode is on by default.

An option to select whether or not to apply the mirror mode is also added to self-portrait shooting. When you turn off the mirror mode in the settings, the photo is reversed left and right and saved as it is actually seen.

6. Run camera first

When you launch the app, it immediately switches to the camera.

Unlike Feica, the Feica City apps focuse on filtering rather than shooting. So the app's main screen is not a camera either. However, some users have sent us feedback that they wanted the option to turn on the camera immediately when the app starts, And we added it because we thought it was a necessary feature.

If you turn on this option in Settings, the app will switch directly to the camera after launch.

With this update, We hope that many of you who use the Feica City apps will be able to use our apps more satisfactorily. Next, we'll come up with a new filter update!
